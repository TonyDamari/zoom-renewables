Power your home with confidence

Zoom Renewables is focused on developing longstanding relationships with landowners across the United States to provide immediate value for their wind/solar rights through up front cash transactions.

Benefits To Landowners

Pay Off Debt

Upfront cash payments for sale of wind/solar rights are a quick source of capital to help satisfy other obligations.

Invest

Proceeds from the transaction will give landowners the opportunity to invest in other profitable projects and ventures.

Buy More Land

Upfront cash proceeds from the sale of wind/solar rights can provide a source of capital to buy more land and expand farming or ranching operations.

Receive Tax Benefits

Proceeds from Stagecoach Renewables's acquisition may be eligible for capital gains tax rather than ordinary income and/or tax deferral through a Section 1031 like-kind exchange.

Simplify Estate Planning

Stagecoach Renewables's all cash offer for the simple division of a lump sum of cash between heirs of an estate.

Retain Surface Ownership

Landowners who sell their wind/solar rights to Stagecoach Renewables will retain ownership of the surface rights.
